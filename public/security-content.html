SECURITY STATEMENT

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

1. INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY

Cloud Infrastructure: VTAC MANAGER is built on top of world-class infrastructure provided by Vercel and Supabase (AWS). Our providers maintain industry-leading certifications, including SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001.

Physical Security: All data is stored in Tier III or Tier IV data centers featuring 24/7 onsite security, biometric access control, and advanced environmental monitoring. We utilize the United Kingdom and United States regions for primary hosting and redundancy.

2. DATA ENCRYPTION

In Transit: All communications between your devices and our platform are encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. This ensures that your tactical data, player information, and login credentials cannot be intercepted by third parties.

At Rest: Our databases and disk storage are encrypted using AES-256, the global standard for industrial-grade data protection. This means that even in the event of physical hardware theft, your data remains unreadable.

3. AUTHENTICATION & ACCESS CONTROL

Supabase Auth: We use advanced authentication systems to manage user identities. Passwords are never stored in plain text; they are hashed using secure cryptographic algorithms to prevent unauthorized reconstruction.

Role-Based Security: We implement Row-Level Security (RLS) at the database level. This ensures that a coach from Team A can never access the tactical data or player CVs of Team B, even if a software bug exists in the application layer.

4. RELIABILITY & BACKUPS

Service Redundancy: Our application is deployed across a global edge network, ensuring high availability and protection against DDoS attacks. Core services target a 99.9% uptime.

Automatic Backups: We perform automated daily backups of all database content. These backups are stored in geographically separate locations to ensure that your tactical library and team history are safe even in catastrophic disaster scenarios.

5. PAYMENTS SECURITY

PCI Compliance: VTAC SOLUTIONS LTD does not store your credit card information on our servers. All financial transactions are handled by PCI-compliant payment gateways such as Stripe or PayPal. They manage the complex security requirements of the payment card industry on our behalf.

6. VULNERABILITY MANAGEMENT

We regularly update our software dependencies and perform security audits of our codebase. If you are a security researcher and believe you have found a vulnerability on our platform, please contact us immediately at security@vtacmanager.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure.
